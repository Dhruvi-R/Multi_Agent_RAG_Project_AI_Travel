* Spend the day relaxing at your hotel, exploring the surrounding area, or taking a sunset stroll along the beach

**Day 2: South Bali**

* Visit the famous Uluwatu Temple, perched on a cliff overlooking the Indian Ocean
* Watch a traditional Kecak fire dance performance at the temple
* Explore the beautiful beaches of Nusa Dua and Jimbaran Bay
* Enjoy a seafood dinner at one of the many restaurants in the area

**Day 3: Ubud**

* Drive to Ubud, a cultural and artistic hub in central Bali (approximately 1.5 hours)
* Visit the Ubud Monkey Forest, a nature reserve and home to over 700 Balinese long-tailed macaques
* Explore the Ubud Art Market, where you can find local handicrafts, paintings, and souvenirs
* Visit the Tegalalang Rice Terrace, a beautiful example of Bali's agricultural landscape

**Day 4: North Bali**

* Drive to the north coast of Bali (approximately 2 hours)
* Visit the stunning Gitgit Waterfall, a 40-meter-high waterfall surrounded by lush tropical forest
* Explore the Lovina Beach area, known for its black sand beaches and dolphin watching opportunities
* Enjoy a relaxing evening in the town of Lovina

**Day 5: Water Sports and Departure**

* Spend the morning snorkeling, diving, or trying other water sports in the Nusa Lembongan Island area (optional)
* Return to your hotel in Seminyak and spend some time relaxing or shopping for souvenirs
* Depart for the airport and your onward flight
